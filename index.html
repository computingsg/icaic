Mission

The International Collegiate AI Contest (ICAIC) inspires university students worldwide to advance in artificial intelligence and computing. Participation is by invitation, with top universities from around the world, including leading institutions in South East Asia, selected to send their best teams. ICAIC provides a platform for students to solve real-world AI challenges using industry datasets, collaborate across borders, and engage in a global dialogue on AI’s opportunities, challenges, and ethical considerations.

Vision

ICAIC aspires to become the premier international AI contest for university students, rivaling the world’s leading collegiate STEM competitions in scope, rigor, and impact. In collaboration with universities, research institutes, and major AI industry partners, ICAIC enables students to work on real-world datasets and practical AI problems. By bringing together emerging innovators from both established programs and developing regions, ICAIC cultivates a global network of AI leaders committed to responsible innovation and societal impact.
